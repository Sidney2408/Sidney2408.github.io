Requirements

First Round: February 26

Your team should submit an executive summary of your business online before March 5.

The executive summary should be about 3 pages long and be split into ten sections:

  1. Company name
  2. Team members (First Name, Last Name, email address, affiliation with SUTD)
  3. Summarize the market and potential creation and capture of value. This should be your elevator pitch. (500 characters with spaces)
  4. Describe the problem and market you are addressing. (1000 characters with spaces)
  5. Describe the value creation proposition. (1000 characters with spaces)
  6. Define the value capture proposition. (1000 characters with spaces)
  7. Who are your potential competitors? (1000 characters with spaces)
  8. Explain how each team member will distinctively help the business venture. (1000 characters with spaces)
  9. What actions or steps will you take to engage with customers and optimize the product-market fit in the next 3-6 months? (1000 characters with spaces)
  10. Optional additional 3 A4 pages of material (PDF upload) to be considered by judges.


Each application will be reviewed by 3-5 outside judges, who will rank applications on three criteria:

  • Value Creation: Has the team clearly identified a significant problem, and compellingly articulated a scalable solution to it?
  • Value Capture: Has the team persuasively articulated how it will earn revenue from the market's adoption of its solution?
  • Credibility: Will the team be able to leverage the prize money to succeed in their plan?
